National Biodiversity Assessment 2025 is coming soon!

The NBA is the primary tool for monitoring and reporting on the state of biodiversity in South Africa, and is produced by the South African National Biodiversity Institute in partnership with numerous institutions and individuals.

National Biodiversity Assessment 2025 is presented in two formats:

  • a Summary of Findings and Key Messages publication (that will be available in print and PDF), and

  • this dedicated website, which will feature around 100 webpages of scientifically referenced, citable content, with links to detailed technical documentation and academic publications.

NBA 2025 will be released in late 2025.
